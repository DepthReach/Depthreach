DepthReach provides B2B and B2C lead generation services including targeted outreach, verified lead delivery, CRM setup, email campaigns, LinkedIn lead generation, and performance reporting. All services are delivered on a monthly subscription basis.

2. Payment Terms

Payment is due at the start of each monthly billing cycle. We accept bank transfers, UPI, and online payment methods. Late payments may result in temporary service suspension. All prices are in Indian Rupees (INR) unless otherwise stated.

3. Lead Quality Guarantee

DepthReach guarantees that all delivered leads are triple-verified. If verifiable lead quality falls below agreed standards, we will replace those leads at no additional cost. Our 30-day result guarantee applies to first-time clients only.

Either party may cancel the engagement with 15 days written notice. No refunds are provided for the current billing cycle. All data collected on behalf of the client remains the client's property and will be returned upon request.

DepthReach treats all client business information, target lists, and campaign data as strictly confidential. We will not share, sell, or disclose any client information to third parties without explicit written consent.

All outreach activities conducted by DepthReach comply with applicable data protection laws. We do not use illegal databases and only contact individuals who may have a legitimate business interest in the client's offerings.
